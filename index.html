Here's about some of the greatest cricketers of India

  • Sachin Tendulkar

    Sachin Tendulkar, called as the God of Cricket, is regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the world with his outstanding 100 tons in his international career, playing for a long period of 24 years of international cricket .He became the first ever batsmen in internatioal cricket to score a double hundred in an ODI match

  • MS Dhoni

    MS Dhoni, also called as the Captain Cool , served as an Indian Captain for an overall period of 13 years. Under his captaincy, team India has won the first ever ICC T20 Trophy in the year 2007, won the ICC Cricket World Cup 2011, and even won the ICC Champions Trophy in the year 2013. He is currently serving as the captain for the CSK franchise in the Indian Premier League

  • Rahul Dravid

    Rahul Dravid, called as the Great Wall of India, is well known for his perfect batting techniques in test cricket. He also served as Indian Captain when the Indian Cricket was facing tough times. Unlike other cricketers, he took the responsibility of serving as a coach for the U19 Indian Cricket team, which performed well under his coaching.He is cuurently working as the director of the National Cricket Academy, India
